About Us

FAMBA is a Zimbabwean company that was formed by a group of university students who saw the rapid increase in the demand of fast and safe means of transportation among the Zimbabweans. The main aim of this company is to provide the safest and reliable means of transportation and these services should be affordable to the general public with different income ratios. FAMBA was first established as an application in 2018 and as the demand increased it was latter formed as a company in 2021 and has been dominating the market ever since.
